The COVENA Trust Framework

A comprehensive verification system ensuring transparency, ethics, and authenticity in beauty brands

What is COVENA?

COVENA (Certified Organic & Verified Ethical Network Alliance) is a rigorous verification framework designed to bring unprecedented transparency to the beauty industry. Every brand on ORDAE undergoes a comprehensive review process to earn their COVENA certification.

We believe consumers deserve to know the truth about where their products come from, how they're made, and whether the reviews they read are genuine. COVENA makes that transparency a reality.

Our Values

ORDAE and the COVENA Trust Framework are built on a foundation of integrity, truth, and accountability. We believe that commerce should be conducted with honesty, that businesses should practice faithful stewardship of their resources and relationships, and that every customer deserves to be treated with respect and transparency.

Why COVENA Matters

The beauty industry has long suffered from opacity. Consumers are told to trust brands without being given the information to make informed decisions. COVENA changes that by:

  • Eliminating greenwashing and false claims
  • Ensuring ethical treatment throughout the supply chain
  • Providing consumers with verified, trustworthy information
  • Creating accountability for brands that claim ethical practices
  • Building a community of brands committed to genuine transparency
